Core Gameplay Mechanics

Vehicles & Priorities

Manage three distinct tiers of traffic, each with unique travel velocities, warning sirens, and paths to test your scheduling timing.

Civilian Vehicles Cars, Taxis, Trucks...
Priority Vehicles Ambulance & Police (Siren warning)
VIP Escort Convoy Strict Police - Limo convoy alignments

Collisions & Penalties

Accidents disrupt operations immediately. Drivers waiting too long at red signals lose patience, shaking their cars before points drain away.

Collision Limits Max 3 lives (Crash Count limit)
Stun Cooldown 1.5 - 2.5s delay recovery post-crash
Impatient Shake Vibrations trigger if waiting > 2 cycles

Dynamic Environments

Adapt to shift-based Day/Night lighting and severe weather cycles. Road friction conditions actively adjust vehicle handling and transit speeds.

Rain Conditions -25% speed drops, tire spray effects
Snow Conditions -20% to -35% speeds, drifting snow flurries
Day & Night Cycles Auto headlights, glowing light beacons
